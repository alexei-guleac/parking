Form hint

Username is required.

  1. At least 5 characters long.
  2. Maximum 15 characters long.
  3. Should contain only A-Z, a-z, 0-9 symbols and "_" or "."

Email is required.

  1. At least 8 characters long.
  2. Maximum 35 characters long.
  3. Should contain @,A-Z, a-z, 0-9 symbols and "_" or "."

Firstname is required.

  1. At least 3 characters long.
  2. Maximum 15 characters long.
  3. Should contain only A-Z, a-z, 0-9 symbols and "_" or "."

Lastname is required.

  1. At least 3 characters long.
  2. Maximum 15 characters long.
  3. Should contain only A-Z, a-z, 0-9 symbols and "_" or "."

Password is required.

  1. At least 6 characters long.
  2. Maximum 10 characters long.
  3. Should contain
    at least one uppercase letter,
    one lowercase letter, one number
    and one special character from !"@#$%^&'*()_+,./
